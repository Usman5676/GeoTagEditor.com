Pro Tips & Best Practices

Expert advice for getting the most out of geotag editing

Finding Coordinates

  • Use Google Maps - right-click any location and select "What's here?" to get coordinates
  • For accuracy, use at least 4-6 decimal places (e.g., 34.052235)
  • Save frequently used locations for batch editing multiple photos

Important Note

Always test with one photo first when batch editing to ensure coordinates are set correctly before processing multiple files.

Best Practices

  • Always keep backups of original photos before editing metadata
  • Remove location data from photos before sharing publicly on social media
  • Regularly update location data for frequently visited places

Efficiency Tip

Create a spreadsheet of common locations with their coordinates to quickly copy-paste when editing multiple photos.
