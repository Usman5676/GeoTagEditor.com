About Image GPS Editor

Image GPS Editor is a free online tool designed for photographers, travelers, and anyone who needs to manage location data in their photos. Whether you want to add missing GPS coordinates, correct inaccurate location data, or protect your privacy by removing location information, our tool makes it simple.

Privacy-First Design

We believe your photos should remain private. That's why our tool works entirely in your browser - your images are never uploaded to any server. All processing happens locally on your device, ensuring complete confidentiality of your personal photos and location data.

Who Is This Tool For?

  • Photographers who want to add location data to their portfolio images
  • Travel bloggers needing to correct or add GPS coordinates to travel photos
  • Privacy-conscious users who want to remove location data before sharing photos online
  • Real estate professionals adding precise location data to property photos

Our mission is to provide a simple, secure, and effective solution for managing GPS metadata in digital images without compromising your privacy.
